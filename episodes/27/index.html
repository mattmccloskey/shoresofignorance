Episode 27

Exploring Character

Episode 27 · April 23, 2020 · 58 min

🎧 Listen on SoundCloud

About This Episode

Matt and Michael open with Michael talking to himself. Then things get interesting. They dive into one of the biggest questions they have ever tackled. Can society actually function without God?

Key Quotes

"To live is a creative act. It's not to recreate something. It is to create something."

— Matt, 04:35

"If there is no God, everything must be permissible."

— Matt (quoting Dostoevsky), 55:12

"The highest good is actually abortion. The highest good is actually transgenderism. The highest good is to put miserable people out of their misery. That right there is an example of how far down the disillusionment goes when you become unmoored."

— Matt, 74:05

"If you substitute anything for God, your world gets smaller. And God is the only thing that if you don't, you can't substitute. The world will not get smaller. It actually gets bigger."

— Michael, 80:00

"I don't have to be God. I can let God be God. I don't have to come up with perfect ideas. I can be creative and say, well, my idea doesn't quite live up. How did it not live up? How can I change that? That's like rest."

— Matt, 78:09
