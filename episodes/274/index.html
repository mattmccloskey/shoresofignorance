About This Episode

Matt and Michael watched Artemis 2 launch into obscurity, then went deeper. They explore the mathematical impossibility of DNA arising by chance: 10^77 odds versus 10^56 atoms in the Milky Way. They discuss self-organized criticality and why systems collapse suddenly. They examine Asimov's Seldon Crisis: the inevitable binary choice point. They contrast Lucifer's "knowledge for myself" with Socratic and Christ-like humility. They unpack nihilism versus meaning through Lord of the Rings archetypes. They examine why David's census brought divine punishment. The seed must die to become a plant. The question is not whether you will die. It is whether you will die for something that matters.
