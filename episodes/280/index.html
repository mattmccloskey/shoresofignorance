Episode 280 · May 21, 2026 · 88 min

About This Episode

Matt and Michael wrestle with one of the oldest questions in philosophy. Why does accepting objective meaning make life harder, not easier?

Key Quotes

"Nihilism is a retreat from meaning. It's not so much a denial of it. It's like RSVPing no to an invitation. You're not saying that the party doesn't exist. You're just saying, I don't want to go."

— Matt, 5:40

"Only people who have tried very hard to be good understand anything about badness."
