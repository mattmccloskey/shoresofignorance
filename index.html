About the Show

Shores of Ignorance is a weekly podcast hosted by Matt McCloskey and Michael Vaclav. Each week we wade into the questions that sit at the edge of what we understand — faith, culture, philosophy, and the ordinary mysteries of being human.

No experts. No tidy answers. Just two friends trying to think out loud.
